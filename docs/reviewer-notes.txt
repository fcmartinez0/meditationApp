Stillness is a sound + breathing app for focus, sleep, and calm.

NO SIGN-IN REQUIRED
There is no account, login, or server. Open the app and everything is
immediately available — no demo credentials are needed.

BACKGROUND AUDIO
The app plays continuous background audio so a session keeps running with the
screen locked (for focus, rest, and sleep). This is why UIBackgroundModes
includes "audio".

NO MICROPHONE / NO RECORDING
All audio is synthesized on-device. The app does not record audio and requests
no microphone access.

PRIVACY
No tracking, no analytics, no ads. All data (preferences, streak, history,
music ratings) stays on the device. Data safety / App Privacy is "Data Not
Collected." Privacy policy: <PUBLIC_PRIVACY_URL>

NOTIFICATIONS ARE OPTIONAL
Daily reminders are off by default. Enabling them in Settings triggers the
standard notification permission prompt. The app is fully functional without
granting notification permission.

WELLNESS, NOT MEDICAL
The binaural/frequency tracks are a wellness aid, not a medical treatment, and
the app states this in-app and in the privacy policy.
