# Stillness — Alternate Positioning (recommended)

Instead of competing head-on with Calm/Headspace as a "meditation app" (where the
missing pillar is voice-guided content), this angle leads with what Stillness is
genuinely best-in-class at: **private, endless, generated focus & sleep sound +
breathing** — no loops, no account, no tracking.

Use this in place of the copy in `store-submission.md` if you want to target the
audience that will actually love the app and leave good reviews.

> Positioning line: *Stillness is a private sound sanctuary for focus, sleep, and
> calm — endless audio generated on your device, never the same loop twice.*

## Why this works better

| "Meditation app" framing | This framing |
| --- | --- |
| Implies guided/voice sessions → users feel a gap | Promises sound + breathing → delivers exactly that |
| Competes with Calm/Headspace (huge, paid, branded) | Competes with white-noise/focus apps (winnable) |
| "Generated audio" sounds like a downgrade vs. recordings | "Never repeats / no loops" is a *feature users feel* |
| Privacy is a footnote | Privacy is a headline differentiator |
| Broad keywords, brutal competition | High-intent keywords (focus, sleep, study, white noise) |

**Net:** you attract people who want precisely what the app does, so first-week
retention and reviews go up — which is what actually drives store ranking.
