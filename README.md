# Stillness

A calm, cross-platform app for **focus, sleep, and rest** — endless generated
sound, guided breathing, and a session timer. Built with Expo (React Native +
TypeScript); one codebase runs on iOS, Android, and the web.

## What it does

- **Session timer** — pick a length, choose a sound, and settle in with a
  breathing "aurora" orb: a glassy gradient core inside slowly revolving
  geometry (a 12-point starburst, hexagons, and a fine tick ring) that paces
  your breath.
- **Guided breathing** — Box, 4-7-8, Calm, and Coherent rhythms with an animated
  pace guide and gentle haptics.
- **A deep sound library**
  - **Ambient:** rain, ocean, forest, stream, campfire, night crickets, brown /
    pink / white noise, and a cat purr.
  - **Frequencies:** binaural-beat tracks (Calm, Clarity, Focus, Dream, Deep).
  - **Beats:** original instrumental grooves in distinct genre styles.
  - **Generative:** endless, never-repeating music that **learns what you like**
    as you rate it.
- **Streaks & progress** — daily streak, total minutes and sessions, weekly chart.
- **Daily reminders** — an optional gentle nudge at a time you choose.
- **Make it yours** — set a custom photo background; lock-screen / Control Center
  artwork while sound plays in the background.

All sounds are generated by the app itself — no recordings or third-party audio.

> Headphones are recommended for the binaural and low-frequency tracks. The
> frequency tracks are a wellness aid, not a medical treatment.

## Sound

Every sound is synthesized procedurally, in stereo, and loudness-matched so
switching between any two is volume-neutral.

### Frequencies

Binaural beats whose carriers sit in the band where the effect is perceived
best, with beat frequencies grounded in entrainment research: theta (~4.6 / 6 Hz)
for relaxation and deep rest, beta (~18 Hz) for focus and working memory.

## Privacy

Stillness is **local-first**: there's no account and no server. Your settings,
streak, history, and music preferences live only on your device, and there's no
tracking, ads, or analytics. A Privacy & Disclaimer page is available in the app
(Settings → About).
