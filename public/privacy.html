Privacy Policy

Stillness · last updated 3 June 2026

Stillness is designed to respect your privacy completely. It is a local-first app: there is no account to create and no server to connect to.

What we collect

Nothing. Stillness does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There is no tracking, no advertising, and no analytics.

What is stored on your device

Your preferences (chosen sounds, volume, reminders), your meditation streak and session history, and the music-taste ratings used by the generative music are stored only on your device. This information never leaves your device and is removed if you delete the app or use “Reset all data” in Settings.

Notifications

If you enable a daily reminder, it is scheduled locally on your device. No notification is sent from any server.

Children

Because Stillness collects no data, it collects no data from children.

Wellness disclaimer

The binaural-beat and frequency tracks are offered as a wellness aid and are not a medical device or treatment. They are not a substitute for professional medical advice. Please consult a healthcare professional for any medical concern.
