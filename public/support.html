Frequently asked questions

Why do some sounds feel "wider" with headphones?

The frequency (binaural) tracks and the ambient sounds are mixed in stereo. Headphones are recommended to hear the full effect; binaural beats in particular only work with headphones.

Does audio keep playing when my screen is locked?

Yes. Sessions continue with the screen off so you can meditate or fall asleep without keeping the phone awake.

How do I turn on (or off) daily reminders?

Go to Settings and enable the daily reminder, then pick a time. You can turn it off there any time. Reminders are scheduled locally on your device — nothing is sent from a server.

What is the "generative" music?

It's an endless, never-repeating score created on your device. Rate tracks you like and dislike, and it adapts to your taste over time. All of this happens locally — your ratings never leave the device.

How do I reset my data?

Settings → Reset all data. This clears your preferences, streak, history, and music ratings from the device. Deleting the app removes everything too.

Are the frequency tracks a medical treatment?

No. The binaural-beat and frequency tracks are offered as a wellness aid only. They are not a medical device or treatment and are not a substitute for professional medical advice.
